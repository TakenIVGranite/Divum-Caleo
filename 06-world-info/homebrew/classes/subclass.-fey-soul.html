Subclass. Fey Soul

Fey Speech

Due to the fey magic that has been infused into your blood you find yourself able to read and speak sylvan.

Fairy Magic

Starting at 1st level, you learn additional spells when you reach certain levels in this class, as shown on the Fey Spells table. Each of these spells counts as a sorcerer spell for you, but it doesn't count against the number of sorcerer spells you know

Fey Spells
Sorcerer Level Spells
1st Charm Person, Tasha's Hideous Laughter
3rd Barkskin, Calm Emotions
5th Hypnotic Pattern, Summon Fey
7th Hallucinatory Terrain, Freedom of Movement
9th Dominate Person, Legend Lore

Silver Tongue

Starting at 1rst level, you can use your action to stare down a creature within 60 feet. That creature must make a wisdom saving through against your spell save DC. If it fails it must use it's next turn to attack the nearest creature to it.

Once you use this feature, you can't use it again until you finish a short or long rest.

Captive Audience

Starting at 6th level, whenever you cast an leveled enchantment or illusion spell you may increase the area the spell effects by 10 feet.

You may only use this ability a number of times equal to your proficiency bonus until you take a long rest.

Fabricate Gateway

Starting at 14th level, you can see the natural fey pathways that connect to this world. Looking at a location up to 45 feet away you can find and use a pathway to get there. Using a bonus action you can open a gateway to this path and hop in. You may also designate an ally size medium or smaller within 5 feet of you to use their reaction to hop in after you. After a creature hops through the gateway they are transported to the end of the path, or within 5 feet of the exit.

Enthralling Spells

Starting at 18th, level the first time a creatures makes a save to end an effect that you placed upon them with an illusion or enchantment spell they automatically fail.
