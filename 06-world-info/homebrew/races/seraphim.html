Seraphim

Other Worldly Appearance

When a person finds themself blessed by a god their form shifts to match that god. This can give a multitude of benefits, but also many visual abnormalities.

Linked To Gods

People of this origin are usually either high clerics of a god themselves or descendants of seraphim. However these traits are not likely being passed down and usually die out in only a couple generations. When a god falls their seraphim loose their powers and stop passing on their blessing.

Seraphim Traits

Age. You reach maturity at whatever age your original race was, however your lifespan has been increased to a millennium.

Size. Your size depends on your race of birth.

Speed Your base walking speed is 30 feet

Godly Magic

You gain the ability to cast spells associated with your god. You know the a cantrip. When you reach 3rd level, you can cast a first level spell once with this trait and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. When you reach 5th level, you can cast a second level spell once with this trait and regain the ability to do so when you finish a long rest. Choose intelligence, wisdom, or charisma for the spellcasting ability of these spells.
